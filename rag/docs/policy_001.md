# Treasury Payment Policy (v1.2)
- Payments > $250,000 require: (1) dual approval (Treasury + Business Unit), (2) counterparty KYC refreshed within 12 months, (3) FX check if non-USD.
- Intra-company payments: approval may be auto if netting rules satisfied and amount < $1,000,000.
- Cut-off time for same-day settlement: 15:30 local.
- Exception handling: transactions with anomaly score > 3.0 must be reviewed by Risk.
